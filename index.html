About Me

I do: filming, video editing, animation, infographics, After Effects, basic 3D and sound design. I work with Adobe software for video and COCKOS Reaper for audio and Blender for 3D.

Among notable commercial projects I did recently is the DeltaLoop introductory proposal video for the DeltaLoop team from Canada. “...succinct and powerful video” according to CanInfra (national innovation competition facilitated by BCG and Brookfield).

And a youtube vlog about construction expo in Finland for russian writer/businessman Sergey Domogatsky. While the first one serves as a good demonstration of basic video editing and infographics the second one has a lot of After Effects graphics and a more modern dynamic approach.
